GitHub Team Plan Pricing (as of April 2026)

✅ Current price: $4 per user per month

This is the standard per-user pricing for GitHub Team plan in 2026, confirmed by multiple reliable sources including CostBench and Get AI Perks pricing guides.

The Team plan includes:
• Organization and team management
• Manage access to projects on a team-by-team or individual user basis
• Unlimited public/private repositories
• Advanced collaboration features

Note: GitHub also offers Free (unlimited repos) and Enterprise ($21/user/month) plans.
