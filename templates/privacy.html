Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At Resynth Inc. ("Resynth", "we", "us"), protecting your privacy is fundamental to how we build our artificial intelligence services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you directly provide to us, as well as data gathered automatically when you use our services:

  • Account Information: Your email address, name, and authentication credentials when you register.
  • Usage Data: Your queries, document uploads, and interactions with the Resynth AI.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and device information automatically recorded by our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly to provide and improve the Resynth AI experience:

  • To operate, maintain, and deliver the core functionality of the Service.
  • To personalize your experience, including saving your chat history.
  • To analyze usage trends and diagnose technical issues.

Important Note on AI Training: We do NOT use your private document uploads or personal chat histories to train our foundational models without your explicit consent.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (such as hosting platforms or authentication providers like Supabase) strictly for the purpose of operating our Service. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We utilize secure encryption for data both in transit and at rest.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may delete your account and all associated chat histories at any time through the Settings menu within the Resynth application.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact our privacy team at privacy@resynthai.com.
